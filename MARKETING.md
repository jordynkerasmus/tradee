# Tradee — Go-To-Market Strategy

## The core challenge: a two-sided marketplace

Tradee only has value when **both** sides are present — but each side needs the
other to show up first:
- **Clients** won't come if there are few tradesmen.
- **Tradesmen** won't stay if there are no clients.

The proven way to crack this is **not** to launch everywhere at once. It's to
**win one small market completely first**, then expand. Get supply (tradesmen)
first, make it look full and credible in one area, then turn on demand.

---

## Step 1 — Pick a beachhead (go narrow, not wide)

Don't launch "South Africa." Launch **one area + a handful of trades** and own it.

- **Suggested area:** start where you have local presence — e.g. **Durban / North
  Coast (KZN)** or your home estate's surrounding suburbs. Local roots = trust + reach.
- **Suggested trades to seed first:** the high-demand, frequently-needed ones —
  **electricians, plumbers, builders/handymen, painters, garden services**. These
  get searched most and have the most available tradesmen.
- **Goal:** ~**20–40 quality listings in that one area** before you market to
  clients. A directory that looks full in one suburb beats one that's empty everywhere.

---

## Step 2 — Recruit founding tradesmen (supply first)

Your **founding-member offer is the hook**: *first 100 get Verified free for 6
months.* Use the urgency ("X of 100 spots left").

**Where to find them (no personal WhatsApp needed):**
- **Local Facebook groups** — community groups, "recommend a tradesman" groups,
  suburb/estate groups, trade-specific groups. Post the recruitment message (below).
- **Trade suppliers & hardware stores** — Builders Warehouse, Cashbuild, plumbing/
  electrical wholesalers. Ask to leave a flyer or chat to contractors there.
- **Existing contacts & word of mouth** — any tradesman who's done work for you or
  people you know. Ask each to refer one more (referral chain).
- **Estate agents, property managers, body corporates, interior designers** — they
  constantly need reliable tradesmen and know many. Partner with them.
- **Gumtree / classifieds** — tradesmen advertising there are actively looking for work.

**The viral loop you've already built in:** every tradesman is told (in the FAQ +
onboarding) to **send their profile link to clients after each job for a review.**
This means each tradesman brings their *own* clients onto Tradee — who then
discover other tradesmen. Lean on this hard; it's your cheapest growth engine.

---

## Step 3 — Make it look credible

- Help the first tradesmen get **2–3 reviews each** (ask them to invite past
  clients). A listing with reviews converts far better than an empty one.
- Personally **verify** the founding tradesmen (the badge) so the area looks trusted.
- Add good photos / complete profiles — offer to help them set it up.

---

## Step 4 — Turn on demand (clients) once supply is ready

Only once your beachhead has enough listings:
- **Local community Facebook groups & estate/neighbourhood WhatsApp groups** — post
  "Looking for a trusted [plumber] in [area]? Find rated local tradesmen at tradee.org."
- **Local Facebook / Instagram ads** — cheap, tightly geo-targeted to your beachhead.
- **SEO** — pages like "find a plumber in Umhlanga" rank over time; the directory
  structure helps. (Future: add location landing pages.)
- **Partnerships** — estate agents and property managers refer their tenants/buyers.

---

## Step 5 — Expand suburb by suburb

Once the first area has tradesmen *and* clients transacting, repeat the playbook in
the next suburb/city. Each new area is easier (you have proof, reviews, testimonials).

## Outreach scripts

### A. Tradesman recruitment — Facebook group post
> 🔧 **Calling all [Durban] tradesmen!**
> Tradee is a new local directory helping homeowners find trusted electricians,
> plumbers, builders & more — and helping you get found and win more work.
> **Founding offer:** the first 100 tradesmen get the **Verified badge FREE for 6
> months** (normally R149/mo). It's free to list, no commission, clients contact
> you directly.
> 👉 List your business in 2 minutes: **tradee.org**
> Spots are limited — get in early. 🇿🇦

### B. Tradesman recruitment — WhatsApp Business / DM (warm)
> Hi [Name] 👋 I run Tradee, a new directory connecting local homeowners with
> trusted tradesmen. I'd love to feature your business. It's free to list, you deal
> with clients directly (no commission), and as a founding member you'd get the
> Verified badge free for 6 months. Can I send you the link to set up your profile?

### C. In-person / supplier pitch (30 seconds)
> "I run Tradee — it's like a trusted local directory for tradesmen. Homeowners
> search by trade and area, see your reviews, and contact you directly. It's free
> to join and right now founding members get verified free for 6 months. Mind if I
> send you the link?"

### D. Client-side post (once your area has enough listings)
> 🏠 Need a reliable tradesman in [area]? Stop asking the group and getting 20
> different names. **Tradee** lists rated, local electricians, plumbers, builders &
> more — with real reviews so you can choose with confidence. Free to use 👉 tradee.org

## WhatsApp setup (without your personal number)
- Get a **dedicated prepaid SIM or eSIM** (~R0–50) and install the free
  **WhatsApp Business** app on it — separate from your personal WhatsApp.
- Use it for **warm, opt-in, 1:1** conversations only. **Never bulk-message
  strangers** — WhatsApp bans that fast.
- For *reach*, use Facebook groups, ads and email; use WhatsApp to *convert*
  interested tradesmen who reply.
- Scale later (if needed) with the WhatsApp Business Platform via a provider
  (360dialog, Wati, Zoko, Respond.io).

---

## First-30-days checklist
1. Get the business WhatsApp number (eSIM + WhatsApp Business).
2. Pick your beachhead area + 4–5 starter trades.
3. Post the recruitment message in 5–10 local Facebook groups.
4. Personally reach out to 20 tradesmen you can find/know.
5. Help the first sign-ups complete profiles + get 2–3 reviews each.
6. Verify founding members (the badge) as they come in.
7. Once ~20–40 listings are live in the area, start client-side posts + a small geo-targeted ad.
